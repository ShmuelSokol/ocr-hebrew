# ksavyad — Hebrew Handwriting Transcription

**Half the price of a typist. Your writings never leave your office.**

Ksavyad reads handwritten Hebrew pages and turns them into clean digital text. A personal AI model is trained on *your* handwriting (or the writer you care about) until it reaches ≥95% accuracy. You review and correct the remaining 5% — and those corrections make it smarter every week.

Everything runs offline, on a thumb drive you plug into your own computer.

## How it works

1. **Week 1** — You send us a sample of the handwriting (on paper or digital). We build your starter model.
2. **Week 2** — You receive your thumb drive. Plug it in, open the app, start transcribing.
3. **Every 1–4 weeks** — As you work, the app saves your corrections. Mail the drive back when you're ready. We retrain and return it.
4. **Over 2–3 cycles** — Your model climbs from ~80% to ≥95% accuracy. From then on, you're mostly just reading.

## Frequently asked

**Can it learn my late father/grandfather/rabbi's handwriting?**
Yes. We need ~20–30 sample pages to start. Accuracy climbs as you correct more pages yourself.

**What if I don't have a computer?**
The app runs on any machine made in the last 8 years. If you don't have one, we can recommend a modest setup under $500.

**What if the thumb drive breaks?**
Call us. We re-issue the drive with your model restored from our archive.

**Can my assistant use it?**
Yes. The license is per machine. Additional machines are 30% of your original setup.

**Do you see my writings?**
Only during retraining, when you mail the drive back. We never receive pages outside that window. Everything you do day-to-day stays on your computer.
